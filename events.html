Extra Curricular Activities

Culture



Choir : A society led by our choir conductor Mr Andile Mvelase. The choir enables students to perform and develop their singing abilities to represent the school in local competitions.


Debate Club : A society which gives students a fun way of developing their oracy skills. There is a range of activities to help with critical thinking and confident communication therefore, allowing students to also be informed about current affairs.


Dance Club : A society which gives students an opportunity to learn dance styles from different cultural backgrounds such as african traditional dance, hip hop dance and salsa dance.


Sports



Soccer Club : A society which gives students a fun way of developing their oracy skills. There is a range of activities to help with critical thinking and confident communication therefore, allowing students to also be informed about current affairs.


Netball Club :To help students with strength and agility. Our core values include RESPECT, TEAMWORK, DISCIPLINE, EXCELLENCE and FUN.


Lastly, we also have a First Aid Team to provide emergency care during matches and tornaments.
