About

History



Between the towns of Khayelistha and Umfuleni, in the Western Cape Province of the Republic of South Africa, is where Mzamomtsha Primary School is situated. Simphiwe Khuze, the school's headmaster, started working there in 2008. The school is situated in Drift Sands, a semi-township that was formerly an unofficial village. Since then, Drift Sands has been improved, and with assistance from the South African government, it has become a vast township with cutting-edge housing complexes. Mzamomtsha Primary School is an important part of Drift Sands and supports the neighborhood.

Curriculum



  • Foundation Phase - (Grade 1-3) The learners will be provided with a strong academic foundation in reading, writing and mathematics
  • Intermidiate Phase - (Grade 4-6) The learners will get a chance to touch on their critical thinking skills with the help of our wonderful educators. In this phase,our learners study IsiXhosa, English, math, life skills, physical education etc.

Policies


Every learner is expected to take 100 % ownership of his/her academic integrity and the caliber of their work. This policy is regarded as a living document that will frequently be updated, All academic staff members, across all departments, should use it as the need develops. Students will act honestly and comport themselves with integrity in accordance with the school's Code of Conduct.
