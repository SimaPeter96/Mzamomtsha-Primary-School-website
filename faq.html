Frequently Asked Questions


What is the admission procedure?
Applications can be completed on-site at the Mzamomtsha Primary School or an online form can completed by contacting the schools email and requesting an application form.

What curriculum will the school follow?
The school will follow the government issued curriculum of CAPS for all school levels from pre-primary to secondary school.

What kind of security measures are offered to protect the pupils at school?
The school has employed multiple security guards which are stationed at each of the school grounds entrances. In addition we have surveilance cameras in most frequented areas of the school and school grounds. In case of emergency, the pupils will participate in emergency evacuation procedures twice a year.

Where can I get information to contact the school?
The schools contact information can be found on our website under Contact, or alternatively, you can click HERE to be directed to the page.

Is Afterschool provided at no cost?
Afterschool is available for all pupils at an additional fee of R800 per annum. Additional information can be found by contcting the school or by visiting our administative offices at Mzamomtsha primary.

Where can I find the school calendar?
The school calendar is issued out to every pupil at the beginning of each school term, or alternatively important dates and events can be found on the Mzamomtsha Primary website under Events.
